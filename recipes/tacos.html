Yummy Tacos

Simple, easy to make, yummy taco recipe

  • 1 lb. Hamburger
  • 2 packets hot taco seasoning
  • 1 head lettuce
  • cheese
  • hot sauce
  • taco shells
  • one cup water
  • taco shells
  1. Cook hamburger in pan until done
  2. drain oil from pan, but do not wash meat
  3. mix water and seasoning into meat. Boil water and stir until well mixed
  4. put completed ingredients into taco shell and enjoy
